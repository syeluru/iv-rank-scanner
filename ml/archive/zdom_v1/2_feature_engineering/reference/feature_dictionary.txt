Temporal Key:
  intraday = computed from today's bars (changes every minute)
  short    = 1-10 day lookback
  medium   = 11-30 day lookback
  long     = 30+ day lookback
  mixed    = ratio or spread across different time windows
  static   = no time component (calendar flag, category, etc.)

Availability Key:
  live   = computed at time of entry from intraday data (changes per minute)
  daily  = known at market open from prior day close
  static = fixed calendar/category flag, doesn't change intraday
